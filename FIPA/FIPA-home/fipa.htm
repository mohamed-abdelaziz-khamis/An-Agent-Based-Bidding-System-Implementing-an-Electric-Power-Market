Welcome to FIPA!

FIPA is a non-profit organisation aimed at producing standards for the interoperation of heterogeneous software agents.

On our site you can find:

  • Information About FIPA,
  • Specifications that FIPA has produced,
  • Activities that FIPA is involved in,
  • Resources relating to FIPA,
  • Special Acknowledgements, and
  • The Member's Lounge.

There is also a FAQ which answers the most common questions regarding FIPA.
